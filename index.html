Latent Tuberculosis

Tuberculosis is a disease caused by germs that spread from to person through the air. TB usually affects the lungs, but it
can also affect other parts of the body. Not everyone infected with TB germs becomes sick.
As a result, 2 TB-related conditions occur: latent TB infection (or inactive TB) and TB disease.

Today, we'll be talking about Latent TB

First, let's start off with how you get Latent TB. Latent TB happens when a person is exposed to TB germs, but they donâ€™t get sick.


Signs And Symptoms

There are no symptoms associated with Latent TB. You feel normal because the germs inside of you aren't affecting you yet. You also can't give TB to others while having Latent TB.


Testing

A way to test for Latent TB is to get a blood test called QuantiFERON done.
